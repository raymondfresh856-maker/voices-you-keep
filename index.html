How It Works

Create and send beautiful voice message cards in 4 simple steps

Privacy Guarantee: Your voice recordings are encrypted and never used for AI training or shared with third parties.
01

Choose Your Theme

Select from 12 beautifully designed card themes perfect for any occasion. Each theme features unique animations, colors, and particle effects. From birthdays to anniversaries, graduations to thank you notes, we have the perfect design waiting for you.

02

Record Your Voice

Use our professional voice studio to record your heartfelt message. See your voice in real-time with our waveform visualizer. Record up to 3 minutes, pause and resume recording, or upload an existing audio file. Add background music to make it even more special.

03

Customize & Preview

Personalize your card with the recipient's name and a custom message. Preview the entire experience including the envelope animation, card reveal, and audio playback. Make sure everything is perfect before sending.

04

Send or Schedule

Share your card via email, text message, or unique link. Generate a QR code for print cards. Or schedule delivery for a future date so it arrives exactly when you want. Your recipient opens an interactive envelope to reveal your voice message with beautiful particle effects.
